# Quick Start Guide
{: .fs-9 }

Get up and running in under 5 minutes!
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

---

## Step 1: Create Your Account
{: .d-inline-block }

1 minute
{: .label .label-green }

1. Go to [trackmeplus.com](https://trackmeplus.com) on your computer or phone
2. Click **Sign Up** (or use **Sign in with Apple** for instant one-tap setup)
3. Enter your name and email, or continue with Apple
4. Click **Create Account**

You're in! TrackMe+ automatically detects your device and loads the right version — desktop on a computer, mobile on a phone.

---

## Step 2: Add Your First Medication
{: .d-inline-block }

2 minutes
{: .label .label-green }

1. Click **My Medications** in the navigation (labeled **Meds** on mobile)
2. Click **Add Medication**
3. Fill in the basics:

| Field | Example |
|:------|:--------|
| Name | Semaglutide |
| Type | Vial |
| Status | Active |
| Dose Amount | 0.5 |
| Dose Unit | mg |
| Frequency | Weekly |
| Time of Day | 09:00 |

{:style="counter-reset:step-counter 3"}
4. Click **Save Medication**

Your medication is now tracking!

---

## Step 3: Log Your First Dose
{: .d-inline-block }

1 minute
{: .label .label-green }

1. Click **Today** in the navigation
2. Find your medication in the schedule table
3. Check the **Done** box — that's it!

Need to log a past dose? Use the date picker on the Today page to navigate back, then check it off.

---

## You're All Set! 🎉

### What's Next?

- **Add more medications** as needed
- **Log doses daily** to build your history
- **Track lab results** — click **Lab Results** (or **Labs** on mobile) to start tracking bloodwork
- **Skip doses** you intentionally miss — keeps your records honest
- **Check your adherence** — go to Reports to see how consistently you're staying on protocol

---

## Quick Tips
{: .text-purple-300 }

| Tip | Description |
|:----|:------------|
| ✅ Quick logging | Check the Done box on the Today page to instantly log doses |
| ⏭️ Skip doses | Use the Skip button to mark doses as intentionally skipped with a reason |
| 🌙 Dark mode | Toggle dark mode in Settings → Display |
| 📱 Install on phone | Add to home screen for app-like access |

## Mobile App
{: .d-inline-block }

Works on your phone!
{: .label .label-purple }

TrackMe+ automatically loads the mobile version when you visit [trackmeplus.com](https://trackmeplus.com) on your phone. For the best experience, install it as a home screen app:

### iPhone/iPad
1. Open **Safari** and go to trackmeplus.com
2. Log in to your account
3. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow)
4. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
5. Tap **Add**

### Android
1. Open **Chrome** and go to trackmeplus.com
2. Log in to your account
3. Tap the **three dots** menu
4. Tap **Add to Home screen**

Now you have a TrackMe+ icon on your home screen that opens like a native app!

# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 1, 2025
**Last Updated:** January 21, 2026

NexHam LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates TrackMe+ ("Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Name (optional)

### 1.2 Health and Tracking Data
- Compound/supplement information you enter
- Dosing schedules and logs
- Lab results you upload or enter
- Vendor and purchase information
- Notes and custom fields

### 1.3 Usage Data
- Login timestamps
- Feature usage patterns
- Browser type and device information
- IP address (for security purposes)

### 1.4 Payment Information
- Billing address
- Payment method details (processed securely through Stripe; we do not store full card numbers)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide the Service
- Store and display your tracking data
- Calculate dosing estimates and inventory
- Send password reset emails
- Process subscription payments

### 2.2 To Improve the Service
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features

### 2.3 To Communicate With You
- Service announcements and updates
- Maintenance notifications
- Subscription and billing information
- Respond to support requests

---

## 3. Information We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal or health data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your health data for marketing purposes
- Access your data except for support purposes (with your permission)

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
Your data is stored on secure servers in the United States.

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement reasonable security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encrypted password storage
- Regular security updates
- Access controls and authentication

### 4.3 No Absolute Guarantee
While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts
We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

### 5.2 Deleted Accounts
When you delete your account:
- Your personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics
- Backups are purged according to our backup rotation schedule

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may retain data longer if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

---

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access Your Data
You can access all your data through the Service at any time.

### 6.2 Export Your Data
You can export your data using the built-in export features.

### 6.3 Correct Your Data
You can edit or correct any data you have entered.

### 6.4 Delete Your Data
You can delete individual records or your entire account.

### 6.5 Data Portability
Your exported data is provided in standard formats (CSV, JSON) for portability.

---

## Account Access Requests

We take your privacy seriously. We will NOT provide access to your account or data to:
- Family members or friends (without your explicit authorization)
- Employers or insurance companies
- Marketing companies or data brokers

We may disclose account information only:
- With your explicit written consent
- In response to valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- To protect the safety of users or others
- To protect our legal rights

If you receive a request for your data, we recommend consulting with a legal professional.

## Getting Started (2 min read)

## Daily Use

### Your Dashboard

This is your home screen. Here's what you'll see:

**At a glance:**
- Your name and a welcome message
- How many medications you're tracking
- How many doses you've logged
- What you've spent on medications

**Today's Schedule:**
Shows all doses due today. Each one has a checkbox. Tap the checkbox when you take your dose.

**Dose Timer:**
Need to wait between doses? Set a timer. Pick 5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes. You'll get a beep when it's done.

**Recent Activity:**
Your last 5 completed doses.

[Screenshot: Dashboard overview]

### Checking Your Schedule

Your schedule shows what's due today. Each medication appears with:
- **Time** - When to take it
- **Name** - Which medication
- **Amount** - How much to take
- **Done** - Checkbox to mark complete

**Taking a dose:**
Just tap the checkbox. Done! The app logs it automatically.

**Viewing other days:**
Tap the date at the top. Pick any day to see that schedule.

**Can't mark future doses:**
You can look at tomorrow's schedule. But you can't check it off until tomorrow.

## Tracking Your Medications

### Adding a New Medication

1. Go to **Medications** in the menu
2. Tap **Add Medication**
3. Fill in the basics:
   - **Name** - What it's called (start typing for suggestions!)
   - **Form** - Vial, pills, drops, patches, etc.
   - **Status** - Active, On Hold, or Completed
   - **Dose** - How much you take
   - **Frequency** - How often (daily, weekly, etc.)
   - **Time** - When to take it

4. Optional extras:
   - **Quantity** - How much you have
   - **Vendor** - Where you bought it
   - **Cost** - What you paid
   - **Notes** - Anything else to remember

5. Tap **Save Medication**

**Pro tip:** Start typing the name. Suggestions pop up from our database. Pick one to auto-fill details.

### Setting Up Schedules

**Frequency options:**
- **Daily** - Once a day
- **Twice Daily** - Morning and evening
- **Weekly** - Once a week
- **Every Other Day** - Alternating days
- **Custom Days** - Pick specific days (Mon, Wed, Fri, etc.)

**Multiple doses per day:**
If you take something twice daily, you'll see two checkboxes. One for each dose. Mark each separately.

### Pre-Mixed Injectables

For medications like Testosterone that come ready to inject:

1. Pick **Vial** as the type
2. Check **Pre-mixed (oil-based)**
3. Enter the concentration (like 200 mg/mL)
4. Enter the vial size in mL
5. Set your dose amount

The app figures out the rest.

### Grouping Multiple Vials

Have several vials of the same thing? Group them together.

**Why group:**
- See your total inventory at a glance
- The app tracks which vial you're using
- Switch to a new vial when one runs out

**How to group:**
1. When adding a medication, find **Medication Group**
2. Enter the base name (like "BPC-157")
3. Do the same for your other vials

**Setting the active vial:**
Tap **Set Active** on the vial you're currently using. That's the one that shows on your schedule.

### Future Start Dates

Not starting a medication yet? No problem.

1. Set a future **Start Date**
2. The dashboard shows "starts in X days"
3. It won't appear on your schedule until that date

Perfect for medications you bought ahead of time.

## Lab Results

Track your blood work and see trends over time.

### Adding Lab Results by Hand

1. Go to **Labs** in the menu
2. Tap **Add Lab Result**
3. Enter:
   - **Test Date**
   - **Test Name** (like "Testosterone" or "A1C")
   - **Result** (the number)
   - **Unit** (like "ng/dL" or "%")
   - **Normal Range** (optional but helpful)
4. Tap **Save**

### Scanning Your Lab Results (Premium)

Don't want to type everything? Take a photo!

**How it works:**
1. Tap the purple **Import from Screenshot** button
2. Take a photo of your lab results
3. Wait a few seconds
4. Check that everything looks right
5. Tap **Import**

**Tips for good photos:**
- Make sure the text is readable, not blurry
- Get the whole results table in the shot
- Good lighting helps a lot

**What it can read:**
- Test names
- Your result numbers
- Normal ranges (if shown)

### Understanding Trend Charts

Want to see how a test changes over time?

1. Go to **Labs**
2. Tap the **Trends** button (looks like a chart)
3. Pick a test from the dropdown
4. See your numbers graphed over time

You need at least 2 results for the same test to see a trend.

### Lab Limits by Plan

| Plan | Lab Results Per Year |

| Free | 10 |
| Standard | 50 |
| Premium | Unlimited |

## Budget & Purchases (Standard+)

Track what you spend on your health.

### Adding a Purchase

1. Go to **Budget** in the menu
2. Tap **Add Entry**
3. Pick a category:
   - Medications
   - Supplies
   - Lab Tests
   - Consultations
   - Shipping
   - Other
4. Enter the amount and date
5. Add a description
6. Tap **Save**

### Scanning Receipts (Premium)

Skip the typing. Just photograph your receipt!

**How it works:**
1. Tap the purple **Import Receipt** button
2. Take a photo of your receipt, invoice, or order confirmation
3. Tap **Extract Purchases**
4. Check that everything looks right
5. Tap **Import**

**What it can read:**
- Items you bought (medications, supplies)
- Prices
- Store name
- Date

**Tips:**
- Screenshots from email work great
- Amazon order confirmations work perfectly
- Make sure text is readable

**Already imported this?**
Don't worry! The app checks for duplicates. Same item, same price, same date, same store? It skips it automatically.

### Vendor Management

Keep track of where you buy things.

1. Go to **Vendors** in the menu
2. Tap **Add Vendor**
3. Enter their info:
   - Name
   - Website
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Notes

When you add medications later, type the vendor name. The app suggests matches and fills in the website automatically.

## Troubleshooting

### My Schedule Is Empty

Check these things:
- Is the medication set to **Active**? (Not "On Hold" or "Completed")
- Is today after the **Start Date**?
- Is today a scheduled day? (If using custom days)

### Timer Notification Didn't Work

1. Tap the ðŸ”” bell icon
2. When your browser asks, tap **Allow**
3. Make sure notifications aren't blocked on your phone

### Numbers Look Wrong

The inventory shows "~" (about) because it's calculated. To fix:
1. Tap **Edit** on the medication
2. Adjust the quantity to match what you actually have
3. Tap **Save**

### Something Else?

- **Email:** support@trackmeplus.com
- Check the help button (?) in the app

## Common Questions

**Can I track any medication?**
Yes! Pills, injections, drops, patches - whatever you take.

**Is my data private?**
Your data is encrypted and only you can see it.

**Can I share my info with my doctor?**
Yes! Export or print reports from any section.

**What if I miss a dose?**
Just log it when you remember. You can backdate entries.

**Does it work offline?**
Yes, for viewing. Changes sync when you reconnect.
